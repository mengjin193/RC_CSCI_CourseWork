We worked to make a user interface (UI) for research project management, logSmart. Since our UI is aimed at serving the needs of academia we consider two types of users: students and professors/research advisers … While Google classroom and Moodle try to create a paperless alternative to class rooms and framework for assignment distribution, LogSmart is directed specifically at student researchers and their faculty advisors. Moreover, our UI has a simpler and more intuitive design that creates a user friendly environment.


- Emad Davis, Project Abstract
