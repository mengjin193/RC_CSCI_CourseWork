News

See what's new with logSmart!

  • April 11th, 2019
    1.0.0. Update

    The logSmart team has finished developing the front end of the website, creating a multitude of pages to make the user experience simple, yet effective. The first few pages are the login, create account, reset password, and forgot password pages. Once the back end is developed, the user will be able to create an account from the create account page, and then login into their account via the login page.

    The main pages of the website include the home and research log page. From the Home page, the user can access every single page on the website including being able to check their deadlines and messages, and sign out. In the Research Log page, the user can update their log by clicking on a date in the calendar, and typing in their update in the text area. They will also be able to edit their log as well as navigate to any other page when needed. Other pages include News, About, Schools, Community, Help, and the users profile page.

    The front end also features some pop-up windows, where the user can click on a certain field and a pop-up window will occur. The pop-up windows include Deadlines, Messages, and Contact Us.

  • April 19th, 2019
    Randolph College Symposium

    The logSmart team will be presenting the fully developed front end of logSmart to the school and community during the symposium at Randolph College. The team will be displaying a poster, explaining how they got started, what has been done, the tools used to develop, and what still needs to be done.
