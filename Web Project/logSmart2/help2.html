Help

Signing In/Creating an Account:

Step 1 :

  • → To login, go to the login page which is located here.
  • → If you already have an account, type in your email and password used when creating your account and click "Sign In".
  • → If do not have an account, click the "Create Account" link located at the bottom right of the login form.

Step 2 :

  • → Once on the "Create Account" page, fill in all the forms completely and validly.
  • → Make sure you have viewed the

    Terms and Conditions.

  • → After completion, click "Create Account" which will bring you back to the login form. Login with your email and password you used to create your account and click "Sign In".

Navigating from the Home Page:

Step 1 :

  • → To get to the home page, either sign in, or click the logSmart logo at the top left of any page. The home page is also available here.
  • → To navigate to any page, click on any tab of the top or bottom navigation bars (top is white, black is bottom). If it is a link to a page, you will be directed to that page in the same tab. If it is a pop-up, the pop-up screen will appear on top of the page you are currently viewing. Clicking the top right "x" or off the pop-up screen will exit the pop-up.
  • → On the home page, you can sign out by clicking the sign out button on the page, or by hovering over "Account" at the top right corner and clicking sign out. Make sure you select "Okay" when the pop-up asks if you are sure you want to sign out.

Step 2 :

  • → The navigation menu on the home page contains four buttons: Research Log, Deadlines, Messages, and Sign Out.
  • → To navigate to the Research log, click the Research Log button, or you can get to the page by clicking here.
  • → The Deadlines and Messages buttons are pop-up windows. Refer to Step-1 on how to use these windows. Again, clicking the Sign Out button will present a pop-up asking if you are sure you want to sign out. Click "Okay" to confirm sign out.

Navigating the Research Log:

Step 1 :

  • → Once you have navigated to the Research Log page, you will see a full-sized calender displayed on your screen. Every other page of the website can be reached from the research log via top and bottom navigational bars.
  • → On the calender, the current day will be highlighted in yellow. To update or edit your research log, click on the calender day in which you want to update or edit. A pop-up screen will be displayed.

Step 2 :

  • → After clicking a calender day and the pop-up is displayed, you will see two sides of the pop-up window. One side displays a text box with an option to update while the other side displays a list of your updates with an option to edit.
  • → To update your log for that calender day, click on the text box and fill in your update. Once you are finished, click the update button and your log will be updated for that day. You will be able to submit multiple updates for the same day.
  • → To edit an update that you have already submitted, click the edit button and proceed to edit your update. Once you are finished, click on the save button to save your edit.
